Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Trace ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information on this website. This site is currently operated as a demand-validation MVP (Minimum Viable Product) prototype to gauge customer interest.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily submit to us via our forms. This includes:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, and shipping address.
  • Preference data: Pre-order customization options and general comments.
Payment Disclaimer: We do not collect, process, or store credit card numbers or payment credentials. All pre-order actions are processed at zero charge as part of an idea validation phase.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is utilized solely to:

  • Evaluate and validate overall interest in the Trace device.
  • Notify you regarding project updates, batch production schedules, or waitlist status.

3. Telemetry and Analytics

We utilize PostHog analytics software to collect anonymized usage details, such as page views and link clicks, to optimize the experience and measure user conversions.

4. Data Sharing and Retention

Your personal information is secure. We do not sell, rent, or distribute your data to third parties. Data is stored securely via integrated storage providers (such as Loops.so and Google Sheets) and will be deleted upon project termination or upon your request.

5. Contact and Opt-Out

If you have questions about this policy or wish to request data deletion, you can contact us at any time at contact@innoveloper.com.
